Retail Store Safety and Customer Service Handbook

1. Employees must clean spills immediately or block the area and report the hazard.
2. If a customer is upset, remain calm, listen respectfully, and contact a supervisor if needed.
3. Employees must never guess about refund policy. They should check the policy or ask a manager.
4. Unsafe actions must be reported to a supervisor immediately.
5. Employees should prioritize customer safety over speed.
6. Employees must not leave sharp tools unattended.
7. In emergencies, call for help and follow store emergency procedures.
